What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners.

Cookies This Website Uses

This website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. It uses very few cookies:

Third-Party Cookies

Google Fonts: This website loads fonts from Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com). Google may set cookies or use other tracking technologies when serving font files. See Google's cookie policy.

GitHub Pages: The hosting provider (GitHub/Microsoft) may set cookies for operational purposes. See GitHub's cookie information.

What This Website Does Not Use

  • No analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, no Cloudflare Analytics)
  • No advertising or marketing cookies
  • No social media tracking cookies or pixels
  • No session or authentication cookies

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites.

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Your Rights

Under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR, you have the right to control what cookies are placed on your device. For more information about your data rights, see the Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the website begins using additional cookies or third-party services. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised.
