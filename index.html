What I Stand For

My priorities as your councillor for Padiham and Burnley West

Council Tax: 7 Years of Maximum Rises: Ended

The Conservatives raised council tax to the legal maximum in 7 of their 8 years in control, including the highest rise in Lancashire’s history at 5.99%. Reform’s first budget delivered 3.80%, the lowest in 12 years.

£28M Inherited Overspend: Nearly Eliminated

When Reform took control, LCC was forecasting a £28 million overspend. Through targeted action, we’ve cut it to £6.1 million and falling, without slashing front-line services.

Care Homes the Conservatives Nearly Closed: Saved

We inherited a consultation process to close five county care homes. After listening to 1,600+ residents, we kept all five open and pledged new investment, because elderly care isn’t a line to cut.

£350M Bond Losses the Conservatives Concealed: Exposed

£519 million invested in VeLTIP bonds now worth an estimated £169 million. The previous Conservative administration failed to transparently report the true scale of these paper losses to councillors or the public.

Reform UK at Lancashire County Council

Changing Lancashire for the Better

In May 2025, Reform won a historic landslide, 53 of 84 seats. In our first year, we’ve delivered a balanced £1.33 billion budget with the lowest council tax rise in 12 years. We corrected a £28 million inherited overspend, saved all five county care homes, and stopped councillors collecting salaries for pension fund directorships.

The previous Conservative administration raised council tax to the maximum in 7 of 8 years, saw savings delivery collapse from 91% to 48%, and left £350 million in concealed bond losses. We’re doing things differently.
