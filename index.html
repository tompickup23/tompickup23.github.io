How It Works

Every council must publish their spending by law. We collect it, analyse it, and make it searchable.

1

We Collect the Data

Every payment over £500 that your council makes is published by law under the Local Government Transparency Code. We download and compile it all — 2.3 million transactions and counting.

2

AI Runs 12 Forensic Checks

Duplicate payments, split payment evasion, Benford’s Law screening, Companies House compliance, supplier concentration analysis, year-end spikes and more — all automated.

3

You Hold Them to Account

Search any supplier, read our audit findings, compare councils, and use the data to ask informed questions at council meetings or through FOI requests.

Trusted & Transparent

Everything on AI DOGE comes from publicly available sources. Here is exactly where.

📜

Official Council Data

Every payment published under the Local Government Transparency Code 2015. Councils are legally required to publish this.

🏢

Companies House

Every supplier cross-referenced against the official UK company register. Active/dissolved status, incorporation dates, director data.

📈

GOV.UK Budgets

Revenue outturn data from MHCLG. Reserves, council tax Band D rates, year-on-year trends. Official government statistics.

🛡

Independent

NOT affiliated with any council. No advertising, no sponsors. Published by Tom Pickup as a public interest project.
